About This Tool

This tool takes your input text, splits it into sentences, and uses a simple word frequency algorithm to select the most important sentences as a summary.

FAQ

  • Q: How does it work?
    A: It splits your text into sentences, scores them based on word frequency, and selects the top sentences as the summary.
  • Q: Can I edit the summary?
    A: Yes, you can copy and modify the generated summary as needed.
