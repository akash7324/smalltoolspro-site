About This Tool

This tool compares two texts using a simple similarity measure based on word sets. It calculates the percentage of common words between the two texts.

FAQ

  • Q: How does it work?
    A: It converts both texts into sets of words and calculates the ratio of the intersection to the union of these sets.
  • Q: Is it completely accurate?
    A: It provides a rough similarity percentage.
