How Crypto Profit Calculation Works

Understand the formula behind cryptocurrency profit calculations

Calculation Formula

Total Investment: Buy Price × Quantity + Buy Fee

Current Value: Current Price × Quantity

Profit/Loss: Current Value - Total Investment - Sell Fee

ROI %: (Profit/Loss ÷ Total Investment) × 100

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about cryptocurrency profit calculation

How is crypto profit calculated?

Profit = (Sell Price × Quantity) - (Buy Price × Quantity) - Transaction Fees. Our calculator automatically includes all these factors.

Are transaction fees important?

Yes! Transaction fees can significantly impact your net profit, especially for frequent trading or small investments.

Can I calculate taxes?

This calculator shows profit/loss, but tax calculations depend on your country's laws and holding period. Consult a tax professional.

What's a good profit percentage?

It varies by market conditions and risk tolerance. Generally, 10-30% annual return is considered good in crypto markets.
