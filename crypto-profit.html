About This Tool

This tool calculates your crypto profit or loss by multiplying the buy and sell prices by the quantity, subtracting any transaction fees.

FAQ

  • Q: How is profit/loss calculated?
    A: It multiplies the prices by the quantity, subtracts fees, and shows the net difference.
  • Q: Can I use this for any cryptocurrency?
    A: Yes, as long as you provide the correct values.
