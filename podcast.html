About This Tool

The Podcast Revenue Calculator estimates your advertising revenue based on your downloads per episode, ad slots, CPM rate, and number of episodes per month.

FAQ

  • Q: How does it calculate revenue?
    A: It calculates revenue per episode and multiplies it by the number of episodes.
  • Q: Is this tool free?
