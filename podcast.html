About This Tool

The Podcast Ad Revenue Calculator is designed to help podcasters and content creators estimate their potential advertising revenue. Simply enter your average downloads per episode, the number of ad slots available, the cost per thousand impressions (CPM), and the number of episodes released per month. The tool then calculates both your revenue per episode and your estimated monthly revenue. Use this information to plan your podcast monetization strategy and negotiate better advertising deals.
