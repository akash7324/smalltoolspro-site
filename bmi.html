About This Tool

The BMI Calculator is designed to help you quickly determine your Body Mass Index (BMI) using your weight and height measurements. BMI is a simple calculation that can indicate whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. This tool provides an easy and free way to track your health status and make informed decisions about your wellness journey.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: What is BMI?
    A: BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a measurement that uses your weight and height to estimate body fat.
  • Q: How is BMI calculated?
    A: BMI is calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by the square of your height in meters.
  • Q: Is BMI a perfect measure of health?
    A: While BMI is a useful screening tool, it does not directly measure body fat or account for factors like muscle mass or distribution of weight.
  • Q: Is this calculator free to use?
    A: Yes, the BMI Calculator is completely free.
