Budgeting Tips & Guidelines

The 50/30/20 Rule

A popular budgeting guideline: 50% of income for needs, 30% for wants, and 20% for savings and debt repayment. Adjust based on your financial goals and location.

Emergency Fund

Aim to save 3-6 months of living expenses in an emergency fund. This provides financial security in case of unexpected events like job loss or medical emergencies.

Track Regularly

Review your budget monthly to identify spending patterns and make adjustments. Regular tracking helps you stay on top of your financial goals and avoid overspending.
