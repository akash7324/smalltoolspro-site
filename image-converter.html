Image Converter

About This Tool

Convert your images from one format to another. Simply upload an image, select the target format (PNG or JPEG), and click "Convert Image." Once converted, a stylish download button will appear below the image for you to save the converted file.

FAQ

  • Q: Which formats are supported?
    A: You can convert images to PNG and JPEG.
  • Q: How do I download the converted image?
    A: After conversion, click the download button that appears below the image.
  • Q: Can I adjust image quality?
    A: Quality adjustments are not available in this basic version.
