Watermark Adder

About This Tool

The Watermark Adder allows you to add a text watermark to your image. Upload an image, enter your watermark text, and click "Add Watermark." The watermark color adjusts automatically for optimal visibility. Once applied, click "Download Watermarked Image" to save the result.

FAQ

  • Q: Can I change the watermark position?
    A: Currently, the watermark is fixed at the bottom right.
  • Q: How do I save the image?
    A: Click the "Download Watermarked Image" button after applying the watermark.
  • Q: Will the watermark always be visible?
    A: The script selects a contrasting color based on the image background.
