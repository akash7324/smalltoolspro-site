Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SmallToolsPro

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.

Can I suggest a new tool to add to SmallToolsPro?

Absolutely! We love hearing tool suggestions from our users. Use the "Tool Suggestion" subject in the contact form above, and we'll consider adding it to our development roadmap.

Are all tools on SmallToolsPro completely free?

Yes! All our tools are 100% free to use with no hidden charges or premium tiers. We believe in providing valuable tools to everyone without barriers.

Do you offer API access for your tools?

Currently, we don't offer public API access, but we're working on it! If you're interested in API access for specific tools, let us know and we'll prioritize development.

Can I use your tools for commercial purposes?

Yes, all our tools are free for personal and commercial use. No attribution required, though we always appreciate mentions!
