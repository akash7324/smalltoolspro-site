Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SmallToolsPro

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.

Can I suggest a new tool to add?

Absolutely! We love hearing tool suggestions from our users. Use the "Tool Suggestion" subject in the contact form above.

Are all tools completely free?

Yes! All our tools are 100% free to use with no hidden charges or premium tiers.

Do you offer API access?

Currently, we don't offer public API access, but we're working on it! Let us know if you're interested.

Can I use tools commercially?

Yes, all tools are free for personal and commercial use. No attribution required.

How often are new tools added?

We add new tools regularly based on user feedback and suggestions. Follow us on social media for updates!
