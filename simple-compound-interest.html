FAQ

  • Q: Which formula is used?
    A: Simple interest uses P×R×T/100 and compound interest uses P(1+R/N)^(N×T).
  • Q: What does frequency mean?
    A: It is how many times per year interest is added (monthly, quarterly, etc.).
