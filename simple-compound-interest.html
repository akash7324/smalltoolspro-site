About This Tool

Compare simple and compound interest for the same principal, rate, and time to understand how compounding grows your money.

FAQ

  • Q: Which formula is used?
    A: Simple: P×R×T/100, Compound: P(1+R/N)^(N×T)
  • Q: What does frequency mean?
    A: How many times per year interest is added (monthly, quarterly, etc.)
